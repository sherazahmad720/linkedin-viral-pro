# CTA Arsenal — 40+ Calls to Action by Goal

> Every post needs a CTA. The CTA determines WHAT TYPE of engagement you get.
> Match your CTA to your goal, not just your content.

---

## Drive Comments (15 CTAs)

### Binary Choice
1. "Team [A] or Team [B]? Drop it below."
   *Best with: Comparison, Hot Take*

2. "Agree or disagree? I'll debate every reply."
   *Best with: Contrarian Take, Unpopular Opinion*

3. "[Option A] or [Option B]? There's only one right answer."
   *Best with: Comparison, Hot Take*

### Fill-in-the-Blank
4. "The best [topic] advice I ever got was ___"
   *Best with: List Post, Story Arc*

5. "Complete this: [Topic] would be better if ___"
   *Best with: Myth Buster, Contrarian Take*

6. "One word to describe your [experience/career/journey]: ___"
   *Best with: Story Arc, Before/After*

### Experience Sharing
7. "What's your version of this story?"
   *Best with: Story Arc, Lessons From Failure*

8. "Has this happened to you? Tell me about it."
   *Best with: Relatable Pain, "This You?"*

9. "What would YOU have done differently?"
   *Best with: Case Study, Story Arc*

### Debate Starters
10. "What's the most overrated [thing] in [industry]?"
    *Best with: Hot Take, Myth Buster*

11. "Name one [thing] you'd remove from [industry] forever."
    *Best with: Contrarian Take, Unpopular Opinion*

12. "What's the hill you'll die on in [industry]?"
    *Best with: Hot Take, Pattern Interrupt*

### Ranking/Adding
13. "What would you add to this list?"
    *Best with: List Post, Framework Reveal*

14. "Rank these from most to least important."
    *Best with: List Post, Comparison*

15. "Which of these surprised you the most?"
    *Best with: Data Drop, List Post*
